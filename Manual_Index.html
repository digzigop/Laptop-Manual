Overview

The Lenovo IdeaPad Slim 3 15ABR8 is a modern, lightweight laptop powered by the AMD Ryzen™ 7 5825U processor and Radeon™ Graphics. Designed for work, study, and entertainment, it offers exceptional performance, energy efficiency, and portability.

Specifications

  • Model: Lenovo IdeaPad Slim 3 15ABR8
  • Processor: AMD Ryzen™ 7 5825U (8 Cores / 16 Threads, up to 4.5GHz Max Boost)
  • Graphics: AMD Radeon™ Graphics (Integrated)
  • Display: 15.6” FHD (1920×1080) Anti-glare, 60Hz, 250 nits, TN or IPS Panel
  • Memory: 16GB DDR4-3200 (8GB Soldered + 8GB SO-DIMM)
  • Storage: 512GB M.2 PCIe Gen 3 NVMe SSD
  • Operating System: Windows 11 Home 64-bit
  • Battery: Integrated 47Wh Li-Polymer, up to 9 hours (Rapid Charge supported)
  • Camera: 720p HD with Privacy Shutter
  • Audio: 2x 1.5W Stereo Speakers with Dolby Audio™
  • Connectivity: Wi-Fi 6 (802.11ax), Bluetooth 5.1
  • Ports:
    • 1x USB-C 3.2 Gen 1 (Data + Power Delivery)
    • 2x USB-A 3.2 Gen 1
    • 1x HDMI 1.4b
    • 1x Headphone / Microphone Combo Jack (3.5mm)
    • 1x SD Card Reader
  • Weight: Approx. 1.6 kg
  • Dimensions: 359.3 × 236.0 × 17.9 mm
  • Color: Arctic Grey

Parts and Features

Power Button: Press to turn your laptop on or off. Hold for 5 seconds to force shut down.

Charging Port: Connect the AC adapter to charge your device. Rapid Charge supported.

Keyboard: Full-sized with function keys and a numeric keypad for productivity.

Touchpad: Multi-touch gesture-enabled touchpad for smooth navigation.

Ports: USB-C, USB-A, HDMI, SD Card Slot, and Audio Combo Jack for versatile connectivity.

HD Webcam: 720p camera with privacy shutter for secure video calls.

Basic Usage

  1. Press the power button to turn on your laptop.
  2. Set up Windows 11 by following the on-screen instructions.
  3. Connect to Wi-Fi via the network icon in the taskbar.
  4. Use the Lenovo Vantage app to manage system updates, battery, and performance settings.
  5. Plug in the charger when battery level drops below 20% for optimal battery health.

Care & Maintenance

  • Keep your laptop in a clean, dry, and cool place when not in use.
  • Clean the screen with a soft microfiber cloth only.
  • Avoid covering the air vents to prevent overheating.
  • Disconnect the charger once the battery is full to extend its lifespan.
  • Regularly back up important files to cloud or external storage.

Troubleshooting

  • Laptop won't turn on: Ensure the charger is plugged in and hold the power button for 10 seconds.
  • No display: Try pressing F7 (Display Switch) or connect an external monitor.
  • Slow performance: Close background apps and check for Windows or driver updates.
  • Keyboard or touchpad not working: Restart your device or reinstall drivers using Lenovo Vantage.
  • Battery drains fast: Lower screen brightness or enable Battery Saver in Windows.
